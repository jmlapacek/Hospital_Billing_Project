Welcome!

This project simulates a medical billing dashboard. The about page describes the team, and additional pages allow for entity updates including patients, bills, payments, and insurance providers. The entity patients include CRUD operations for adding, editing, and deleting records. The data visualiation page includes the following charts: a pie chart that shows a breakdown of what payment methods were used, a bar chart showing billing status, and a line chart showing the revenue over a given time period. The dashboard is designed to be interactive.
